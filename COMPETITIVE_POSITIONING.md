**What They Do Well:**

**Your Advantage:**
- ‚úÖ Works with YNAB's CSV format
- ‚úÖ Doesn't require users to leave YNAB
- ‚úÖ Solves the #1 pain point without changing their workflow
- ‚úÖ Especially valuable when bank connections break

**Positioning Against YNAB:**

- **Message:** "Stop reviewing. Start trusting."

**Key Differentiators:**
| Feature | Monarch | Your Tool |

- May undervalue for heavy users

#### Option B: Monthly Subscription (Low)
- $5-7/month
- Unlimited CSV uploads
- Position as "cheaper than Tiller, easier than Monarch"

- Predictable revenue
- Lower than competitors

**Cons:**

- Competes with existing tool costs

- Free: First 100 transactions per month
- $5/month: Unlimited transactions + AI training
- $10/month: + API access for integrations

**Pros:**
- Low barrier to try
- Land-and-expand strategy
- Can build user base quickly

**Cons:**
- Free tier might cannibalize paid
- Support costs for free users

#### Option D: Pay-Per-Value (Recommended)
- Free: Upload and see AI suggestions (no export)
- $3: Export categorized CSV (one-time per file)
- $9/month: Unlimited uploads + AI improvement

**Pros:**
- Try before you buy
- Users see value before paying
